Hi friends, this is Yang.
I'm an undergrad studying Mathematics and Music Composition, while juggling between multiple jobs as a research assistant (and enjoying it very much!).

Projects that I'm working on:
Hippocampus/Cognitive Map modeling with James Whittington and Jo Warren
Naturalistic RL agent analysis with Ryan Badman in Kanaka Rajan lab
Episodic Simulation modeling with Marcelo Mattar
Inter-regional communication between M1, M2 and S1 with Yu-Wei Wu

My research interests lie between theoretical neuroscience and computational cognitive science.

I want to understand how human imagination/mental simulation works. Memory's usage in imagination/mental simulation is a direction I'm trying to push, as I think it has been largely neglected in current research.

I believe offline learning is a good way to frame it, and hippocampal replay is a good angle to cut in.

I'm also hugely interested in memory retrieval, for one cannot imagine without retrieving one's memories and potentially recombining them.

Other interests include philosophy of science and neuroscience, privacy, environmental protection, motorsport strategy, basketball strategy, and esports strategy.
