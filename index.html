Hi friends, this is Yang.
An undergrad studying Mathematics and Music Composition, while juggling multiple research assistant jobs (and enjoying it very much!).

Projects that I'm working on:
Hippocampus/Cognitive Map modeling with James Whittington and Jo Warren
Naturalistic RL agent analysis with Ryan Badman in Kanaka Rajan lab
Episodic Simulation modeling with Marcelo Mattar

My research interests lie between theoretical neuroscience and computational cognitive science.

My goal is to understand how human imagination/mental simulation works using neural networks and computational cognitive models. I focus on the use of memory in imagination/mental simulation, as I think it has been neglected in previous research.

I believe mental game engine and offline learning are two good ways to frame imagination/mental simulation, and hippocampal replay could be a candidate neural process.

Memory retrieval is another major interest of mine, for one cannot imagine without retrieving one's memories and potentially recombining them.

Other interests include philosophy of science and neuroscience, privacy, environmental protection, motorsport strategy, basketball strategy, and esports strategy.
